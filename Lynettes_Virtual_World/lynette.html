VHS umami pop-up trust fund

Thanks to Nomura colleagues who are members of our Friends of Rays of Sunshine scheme and make a monthly donation of £10, the charity is able to grant more wishes of seriously ill children and young adults, like Toby.

Toby, who is 18 years old, was diagnosed with cancer of the kidney at a very young age. He eventually lost his kidney and had a heart transplant in September 2013. Toby has wanted to be an actor for as long as he can remember and as he has always been a fan of the TV series Blackadder, his wish was to meet actor Stephen Fry. With funds donated by Nomura Friends his wish came true.
